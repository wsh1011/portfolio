ABOUT

  • Who We Are

    OFFICIAL TICKET OFFICE for the BERNINA RED TRAIN

    We are a Tour Operator and Official Ticket Office for the famous Bernina red train.

    Thanks to us, every year more than 15000 passengers leave without queuing and with the right information to enjoy the best experience on the wonderful route along the Swiss Alps.

    Since 2010 we organize in the finest detail personalized trips in Valtellina, both in hotels and in B&Bs, and excursions aboard the UNESCO Bernina Red Train in nearby Switzerland for groups and individuals alike.

  • Our Mission

    We'll guide you through wine-tours in the best wineries and tasting unforgettable traditional dishes alongside our local experts, and we'll help you discover the finest trekking and/or biking routes in the heart of the Alps.

FAQ

  • QWhat is the Bernina Red Train?

    AThe Bernina Red Train is a train connecting St.Moritz in Switzerland and Tirano in Italy, by crossing the Swiss Alps

  • QWhere does the Bernina Red Train depart from?

    AThe Bernina Red Train departs from the stations of Tirano (Italy), of St.Moritz, and of Chur (Switzerland).

  • QWhen does the Bernina Red Train depart?

    AOn the St.Moritz line, the Bernina Red Train departs at various times throughout the day both from Tirano and from St.Moritz.

  • QHow much do the tickets cost?

    ATicket costs can be consulted by selecting the desired route directly from the online ticket office
